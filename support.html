Support

Support for Morla is available to all via email: baku _at_ morlardf _dot_ net.
If you're lucky, IRC is potentially the fastest way to get an answer. The official channel is #morla on freenode.net.

Commercial Support

Commercial users of Morla are strongly encouraged to purchase a commercial support contract, please contact me for a quotation. I will always prioritise support requests from those with support contracts.

Donations

Users not purchasing commercial support are encouraged (but not obliged) to make a donation. By donating you are supporting the principle of open source software and you will help ensure continued development of morla.
